Logline

You are Lucas.

What starts as a picnic collapses into confrontation when Janaye forces a reckoning — then armed strangers pull the night into a warehouse where silence, confession, and courage all cost something different.

Right Here Right Now! is proof that interactive live-action cinema can ship: authored as a story graph, captured in 360°, assembled in Unity with the Alacrity Player, and released to headsets people already own.

Every choice echoes.

The format

Not a menu. A night that branches.

Confess
Protect
Run

V1 ships 16 scene nodes across 22 paths and three emotional tracks. V2 targets 60+ nodes and a feature-length runtime — same pipeline, more graph.

Playback is Meta Quest–ready, VRC verified, with ambisonic / 7.1 binaural sound designed for headset presence rather than flat-screen afterthought.

Pipeline

How interactive cinema actually ships.

Capture

Insta360 Titan + Canon R

Shot up to 11K, presented in 4K 360° — live-action plates, not generated filler.

Author

Story graph → JSON VR bundle

Branching authored in Pure Alacrity / Alacrity Hub, color in DaVinci, packaged for the Alacrity Player.

Deliver

Unity · Quest · SideQuest

Headset build live; free SideQuest V0.0.1 available; Meta Horizon distribution in market. Seeking serious production partners for the larger cut.
